Frequently asked questions

  • What home services does NovoFix offer in Miami?

    NovoFix connects you with certified professionals in Miami, FL for handyman, plumbing, residential electrical work (including 24/7 emergencies and NEC code compliance), AC technicians, home remodeling, preventive maintenance and more. We cover Miami-Dade, Broward and Palm Beach.

  • Are the professionals verified and certified?

    Yes. We validate identity, contact information and experience for each professional. Technical services such as electrical and AC are performed by certified pros with guaranteed services.

  • How does the free online quote work?

    You post your request describing the job (photos optional). Within minutes you'll receive proposals from local pros with price, ETA and reviews. Compare and pick the best fit at no upfront cost.

  • Do you offer 24/7 residential electricians?

    Yes. Miami electricians are available 24/7 for emergencies: power outages, tripping panels, NEC-compliant installations and residential electrical panel sizing.

  • Can I connect with local contractors for a remodel?

    Absolutely. NovoFix works as a Florida contractor directory with experience in Miami home remodeling: kitchens, bathrooms, floors, paint and additions. Get comparable quotes with no commitment.
